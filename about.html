Who We Are

The Alliance for Democracy

The Alliance for Democracy (AFORD) is one of Malawi's oldest and most principled political parties, founded at a defining moment in the nation's democratic journey. Since its establishment, AFORD has remained committed to building a Malawi where every citizen — regardless of region, tribe, gender, or economic standing — has an equal voice and an equal chance.

Our Mission

What We Stand For

To advance democracy, justice, and inclusive development by empowering citizens, protecting rights, and delivering policies that improve everyday life for all Malawians.

We work through grassroots engagement, bold policies, and leadership that listens, acts, and delivers real results where they're needed most.

Our Vision

The Malawi We See

A united, self-reliant Malawi built on equity, opportunity, and dignity - where every citizen has a voice, a stake, and a future.

We see a Malawi where opportunity is shared, dignity is protected, and progress is powered by every citizen - not just a few.

Our Core Values

Democracy

Every Malawian deserves a voice in the decisions that shape their life.

Integrity

We hold ourselves to the highest standards of honesty and transparency.

Inclusion

No region, gender, or community is left out of our vision for Malawi.

Courage

We speak difficult truths and make hard decisions in service of the people.

Community

Our policies are shaped by grassroots voices, not boardrooms or elites.

Accountability

We answer to the people of Malawi — in elections and every day between them.
